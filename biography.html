Biography

Morgan Gil is an artist based in Plettenberg Bay, South Africa.

Morgan Gil works across photography, film, field research, landscape, built fragments, and systems of place. His practice is concerned with the interface between bodies, terrain, tools, architecture, and ecological conditions.

The work draws on travel, outdoor practice, construction, research, and direct observation. It treats the field as both subject and method: a place for images, structures, protocols, and forms of attention to emerge.

Current lines of work include coastal field studies, process based image making, speculative infrastructure, and artist led research into land, access, stewardship, and networked forms of ownership.

This biography is a working text and should be edited as the public body of work becomes more specific.
